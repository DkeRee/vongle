Hey! Thanks for checking out my platformer, Vongle! As you can see, I'm not too great at making names for my games, but hey, at least it's original. I wish that I could've made it in pure JavaScript but I had to in order for it to be qualified for my school's coding competition. The prize is a large rainbow owl and I have grinded my ass off to get it. I have no clue what the outcome will be, for the end date is December 8th, but until then feel free to give it a go. Begin your journey by clicking on the green flag...
